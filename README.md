# Swiggy-Analytics
I Have Built an interactive Swiggy dashboard to track orders, revenue, and delivery performance
Analyzed customer ordering patterns and peak time behavior
Identified top-performing restaurants contributing maximum revenue
Monitored on-time delivery % and order cancellations
Evaluated customer ratings to measure service quality
Provided insights to improve delivery efficiency and reduce cancellations
Used tools like Excel / Power BI / Tableau / MySQL for data analysis and visualization

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Orders Trend
Total orders show overall business growth
Identify peak days / months (weekends usually higher)
Low-order days can indicate demand gaps

2. Top Performing Restaurants
Few restaurants generate majority of orders
Top 10 contribute most revenue

3. Customer Behavior
Repeat customers contribute a large share
New customers fluctuate month to month

4. Delivery Performance
On-time delivery % is a key KPI
Delays may happen during peak hours

5. Order Status Analysis
Completed orders are highest
Cancelled orders still noticeable

6. Ratings & Feedback
Most ratings are positive (4–5 stars)
Low ratings linked with delays or food quality

7. Revenue Insights
Revenue increases with order volume
High-value orders come from specific locations/restaurants

8. Peak Ordering Time
Lunch & dinner time = highest orders
Late-night orders are lower

👉 Insight: 
1. Optimize delivery agents during peak hours
2. Business is seasonal → focus more on high-demand days
3. Focus marketing on high-value areas
4. Delivery time directly impacts customer satisfaction
5. Need to reduce cancellations → improves revenue
6. Delivery efficiency drops when demand increases
7.  Business depends heavily on top partners → maintain strong relationships
8.  Business is seasonal we have focus more on high-demand days
